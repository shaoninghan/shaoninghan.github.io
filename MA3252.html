Course Description

MA3252 is a 4-unit course designed for undergraduate students in mathematics. The objective of this course is to work on optimization problems whichcan be formulated as linear and network optimization problems. It covers a variety of topics: LP modeling; Geometry of LP; Development of simplex method; Duality theory; Sensitivity analysis; Network optimization problems and the network simplex method. Lectures are based on course notes which will be posted on Canvas. There is no required textbook. Lectuers will be recorded and uploaded to Canvas>Videos/Panopto.
