Course Description

ISE 530 is a core course for Master in Data Analytics. Topics covered include: fundamentals of linear programming, integer programming, quadratic and nonlinear programming for analytics and data science problems. NOTE: Recommended Preparation: Multivariable calculus on the level of MATH 229, linear algebra on the level of MATH 245, computer programming on the level of ISE 150. Syllabus can be found here.
