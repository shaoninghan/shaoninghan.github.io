Open Positions

Postdoc

I am recruiting one postdoctoral research fellow in mathematical optimization through NUS (Suzhou) Research Institute. If you are interested in working with me, please contact me and include your CV.

  • The postdoc will be based in Suzhou, China for most of the appointment.

  • The appointment length is approximately 2.5 years.

  • The monthly salary is in the range of CNY 20,000-25,000. Additional financial support will be provided as needed (e.g., overseas travel support).

  • Requirements: Applicants with a background in any area of mathematical optimization are welcome. Prior research experience in mixed-integer optimization or nonsmooth optimization is a strong plus.

Ph.D. Student

If you are applying for a Ph.D. at NUS, please apply directly through the NUS Mathematics graduate program or the NUS IORA program, as admissions are handled in a centralised manner.

If you are currently a Ph.D. student and are interested in working under my supervision, please email me directly with your CV and transcripts.

Undergraduate

If you are a NUS undergraduate student seeking a supervisor for the Undergraduate Research Opportunities Programme in Science (UROPS) or the Final Year Project (FYP), you are welcome to contact me. The minimum requirement is completion of at least one optimization course (MA3236, MA3252, or a more advanced module).
