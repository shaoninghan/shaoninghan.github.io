Biography

Shaoning is currently a 4th year Ph.D. student under the supervision of Professor Andrés Gómez. Shaoning received his B.S in Mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He is expected to obtain his Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering in 2022. Shaoning's research is focused on nonlinear optimization and mixed-integer programming.
